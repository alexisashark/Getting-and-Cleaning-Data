"tBodyAcc.mean...Y":     indicates the mean value of body acceleration signals in Y axis
"tBodyAcc.mean...Z":     indicates the mean value of body acceleration signals in Z axis
"tBodyAcc.std...X":        indicates the standard deviation of body acceleration signals in X axis
"tBodyAcc.std...Y":        indicates the standard deviation of body acceleration signals in Y axis
"tBodyAcc.std...Z":        indicates the standard deviation of body acceleration signals in Z axis
